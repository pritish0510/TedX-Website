### TEDx Sponsorship Guidelines (Compliance)
We strictly adhere to TEDx sponsorship rules to maintain the integrity of our platform:

1.  **No Editorial Control:** Sponsors have no influence over the program curation, speaker selection, or content.
2.  **No Stage Time:** Sponsors are not permitted to present from the stage. The stage is reserved for "Ideas Worth Spreading."
3.  **Logo Usage:** Sponsor logos will be displayed on slides during breaks and on event materials, but never on the red circle carpet.
4.  **Separation:** Commercial messaging is kept separate from the editorial content.
